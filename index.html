noun

welcome to Campus Callosum

Named after the tissue that connects the left and right hemispheres of the human brain, Campus Callosum exists to connect people with different views and beliefs across UTK's campus. It is our mission to create a space conducive to free expression of ideas, foster meaningful conversation, and engage in the multitude of issues affecting our generation.

what this club is

  1. An open space for people to speak without judgement.
  2. A think-tank for solutions to local and global problems.
  3. A place of expression for all types of thoughts and beliefs.
  4. A resource to broaden understanding for different points of view.
